About Me

Hi! My name is Tair Sarsenov and I am a 15 year old golfer from Almaty, Kazakhstan. Last year I moved to the USA, where I am a rising sophomore at the Peddie School in New Jersey.

I have been playing golf since I was 6 years old, when I followed my big brother to the golf course and begged him to let me hit some balls. Since then, I have been hooked and, thankfully, I've improved a lot since I first destroyed the green with my brother.

Since I started playing, I have taken every opportunity to improve my game including: training bootcamps at IMG Academy, lessons at Core Golf Academy, and, this year, I was fortunate enough to receive coaching from golfing legend - Butch Harmon.

Golf is my passion and it is my ambition to become a top player and eventually a college recruit.

I hope you enjoy my site!

Tair

Goals

Golf is really important to me and one of the best parts about being a golfer is that there is always something to learn. I am always motivated to improve and can continue to develop my game throughout my life.

My short-term goals in golf are quite simple - I want to continue playing for my school team and hopefully become Team Captain one day. Playing as part of a team is an amazing experience and I love the camaraderie of being part of a group with a common goal. We challenge each other and push ourselves to be the best players that we can be.

In the future I hope to be a successful college golf recruit at a Division I university and continue to improve whilst pursuing a college education. I strongly believe that golf has already equipped me with the dedication and determination to succeed.
